Background

I grew up as a suburban kid, born and raised in Aurora, IL. The second largest city in Illinois. It was there I attended West Aurora High School and discovered my passion for programming and computer science.

I took a C++ class to learn the fundementals of programming. From there I began teaching myself. I started a software development company known as Rogue Labs at 16. It was successful to the point that I had no need for a part-time job while in school.

I ran the business until the end of my freshman year at the Illinois Institute of Technology in Chicago. That's when I sold the business to my close partner so that I could focus on my studies and becoming a young professional.
